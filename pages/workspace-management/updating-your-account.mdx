# Updating your account

You can perform basic account updates like changing your email address, password, and personal/team name from the "Account" tab in your Rulebricks navbar.

import { Steps } from 'nextra/components'

<Steps>

### Open the account menu

To update your account information, find the navbar in the far top right corner of the Rulebricks dashboard, and click "Account".

![Account Tab](/static/images/workspace-management/workspace-customer-portal-account.png)

### Edit your account information

Here, you can update your email address, password, and personal/team name.

![Account Tab](/static/images/workspace-management/updating-account-info.png)

</Steps>
